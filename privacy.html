Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
Sanctuary does not sell your personal data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

Sanctuary is a science-based sound therapy and neural rest application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Usage data: Sound sessions used, duration, and frequency preferences — to personalize your experience.
  • Device data: Device type and OS version for compatibility.
  • AI interaction data: Text entered for AI-guided sessions is sent to Google Gemini API. Not stored by us.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • No name, email, or identifying information unless voluntarily provided.
  • No microphone, camera, or contacts access.
  • No location tracking.
  • No data sharing with advertisers.

4. Google Gemini API

AI features send your input to Google's servers under Google's Privacy Policy. We do not store this data.

5. Subscriptions and Payments

All payments processed through Google Play Billing or Apple App Store. We do not store payment information.

  • Monthly: $9.99/month
  • Annual: $59.00/year
  • Cancel anytime through your store account settings.

6. Data Storage

Preferences and session history stored locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app deletes all local data.

7. Children's Privacy

Sanctuary is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of significant changes within the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Questions or data deletion requests: sanctuary.app.contact@gmail.com
